Sunset Raspberry

This beer is a tribute to Arne Eriksson. Whos nickname was Rishagar´n. He was born and raised in Rishagen and lived all his life in the same house He didn´t complicate things. He liked to do things properly without frills (krussiduller).

Rishagar´n is a regular lager beer witha light scent of orange peel and a hint of lavender. This lager is made in Arne´s spirit. It is brewed in a simple but solid way. Raise your glass and make a toast for Rishagar´n.
